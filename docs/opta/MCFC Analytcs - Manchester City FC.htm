Joining The Analytics Community

You have a unique opportunity to be part of this community.  Register your interest in this project and we will send you our OPTA data set for every on the ball event for every Premier League player in every match in the entire 2011-12 Premier League season FOR FREE.

This data is designed to be broken down, analysed, graphed and visualised however you see fit.  We have made it available to encourage and inspire the next level of analytics.  Our hope is that the data is used to create new performance measures, tools for player/team comparison and profiling, season-long analysis for benchmarking players performance and contextualising these performances based on playing position and opposition.

Below are just a very few of the areas that we at Manchester City would be interested in discussing with you and in which we feel the area of performance analytics requires further development:

  • Position specific metrics to accurately measure performance and allow for player comparison
  • Seasonal player profiling
  • Data visualisation

 

Once we email you the data, why not share your work with us?  We will work directly with those of you who come up with good concepts and also connect you with others who are working in the same research area.  A collective approach will help the community to make more rapid progress and we want you to  join us in being part of it.

We will be running a research competition in the coming months whereby the work you submit to us will be reviewed by our Performance Analysis department and OPTA with the best projects being published on our MCFC Analytics page and the OPTAPro website to share with the world.

Furthermore, as recognition of your contribution to the performance analytics discipline we will invite those with the best projects to come to our training ground to present their work and to then share with us the match day experience, observing how the Performance Analysis department supports our first team during a Premier League game.

Register Your Interest

MCFC Analytics is a project we are launching for this very reason, to share the data that we have with the analytics community, and further to that, engage directly with the community to see where the journey takes us all. The data is not click to download for security purposes you will recieve the data from mcfcanalytics@mcfc.co.uk within 24 hours over the launch weekend, after which data will be sent every 48 hours.

Please read the Data Available section before completing this form to ensure you register for the most relevant data set.
